About the Lagos State University CSC Library

The Lagos State University CSC Library is a digital library designed to meet the needs of Computer Science students at LASU. We offer easy access to a wide collection of digital books and resources relevant to Computer Science.

Our mission is to empower students with essential knowledge and digital tools that enhance academic and career success in the ever-evolving tech world.

  • 24/7 access to digital resources
  • Simple and fast search tools
  • Updated content reflecting current trends
  • Supportive digital learning environment

We continually improve our services to serve you better. If you have questions or suggestions, please reach out.
